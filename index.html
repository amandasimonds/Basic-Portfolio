About Me

Front-End Web Developer with a background in art education, passionate about helping people and creating beautifully intuitive and inspiring user experiences on the web.

Recently earned a certificate in Full Stack Development from University of Denver. Freshly learned skills in JavaScript, CSS, HTML, ReactJS, CSS frameworks, MySQL and MongoDB, and responsive web design.

A creative problem-solver, I intend to engage audiences of different abilities in achieving goals and being educated through effective user experiences. In my team projects, I combined my skills as a web developer with my background in education to create these types of programs. One of these projects, SpacePort, is an app that gives the user a fun, bright experience browsing resources about Space. We designed the interface to draw people in and make learning fun. In addition to SpacePort, we created a sister app, OceanPort, that also engages users in learning, through a variety of interactions. Utilizing my experience with children, I created the user badge system, to keep track of progress and achievements. My goal is to make learning as fun and interactive as possible


Inspired by my experience in education, and passionate about problem-solving and coding, I am excited to work with more innovative people to build fun and educational experiences on the web.
